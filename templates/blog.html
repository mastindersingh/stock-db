Key Financial Terms Explained

ROCE (Return on Capital Employed)

This is a financial ratio that measures a company's profitability and the efficiency with which its capital is employed. ROCE is calculated as Earnings Before Interest and Tax (EBIT) divided by Capital Employed.

Cash and Cash Equivalents

Highly liquid assets including currency, short-term investments, and treasury bills.
